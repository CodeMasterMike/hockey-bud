Playing Style

A consummate two-way forward with exceptional vision and playmaking ability. Giroux combines elite faceoff skills with a relentless compete level, equally dangerous on the power play and penalty kill. His ability to read developing plays and thread passes through traffic has made him one of the most productive setup men of his generation. A natural leader who captained Philadelphia for nearly a decade, he brings veteran savvy and positional intelligence that elevate the play of his linemates. Despite a shift to a complementary role in Ottawa, his hockey IQ and puck-handling remain among the best in the league.
