Playoffs (Standings tab — appears two days after regular season ends):
Two days after the regular season concludes, a new "Playoffs" tab becomes the default in the Standings section. The regular season standings remain accessible in a second tab. The Playoffs tab displays bracket views with three sub-tabs: Eastern Conference (default), Western Conference, and Full League. Each matchup box within the bracket lists the team's conference playoff seed and their regular season record. Series scores update live during games. Clicking any matchup box navigates the user to a dedicated matchup detail page.

The matchup detail page is split into two horizontal sections. On the left, display the teams' head-to-head regular season stats including: regular season record vs each other, GF, GA, Sv%, PP%, PK%, shots per game, shots against per game and FO%. Beneath the regular season H2H, provide a brief matchup summary covering each team's strengths and weaknesses, a description of how the favored team is expected to win, and how the underdog could create an upset — with the understanding that anything can happen in playoff hockey and regular season tendencies do not always carry forward. For all rounds after the first, insert a "Current Playoff Stats" section between the regular season H2H and the matchup summary showing each team's accumulated stats for these playoffs only. On the right, display each team's all-time playoff stats using the same stat categories but with all-time playoff record instead of regular season record. Beneath that, display the all-time head-to-head playoff stats between the two teams.


Off-Season Schedule (default landing page during off-season):
During the off-season, the Schedule tab becomes the default landing page for the entire site. The score bar displays the next major upcoming event instead of game scores. The page shows a chronological timeline of all important off-season dates and deadlines including: buyout windows, qualifying offer deadlines, expansion draft, NHL Entry Draft (Round 1 and Rounds 2-7), free agency opening, second buyout window, salary arbitration filing deadline and hearings, prospect tournaments, training camp, preseason, roster deadline, and regular season opening night. Important deadlines and events are visually highlighted. Countdown cards at the top show days remaining until the next major event, free agency, and the season opener.


Draft (replaces Scores tab during off-season):
During the off-season, a "Draft" tab replaces the "Scores" tab in the navigation. On draft days, selections update live as picks are made. The score bar ticker shows completed picks scrolling with the current "on the clock" team highlighted. The draft page displays selections in a table organized by round tabs (Rounds 1 through 7). Each row shows the pick number, selecting team (with logo), player name, position, previous club/league, and nationality. Every selected player's name is clickable and navigates to their player profile. During the live draft, the current pick shows an "on the clock" indicator and pending picks appear dimmed. After the draft concludes, the full results remain browsable by round throughout the off-season until the regular season begins and the Scores tab returns.
